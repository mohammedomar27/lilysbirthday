Getting there

🚢 Boarding

Rowes Wharf, Gate A

Happy departs from Rowes Wharf, adjacent to Gate A — underneath the large archway next to the Boston Harbor Hotel, and to the left of the Wharf Room.

🅿️ Parking

30 Rowes Wharf, Boston MA

Park at the Rowes Wharf Garage — check their website for rates. Take the elevator to ground level, head outside, and follow the archway left of Gate A to find the boat.
